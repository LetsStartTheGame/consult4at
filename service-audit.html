Our services

Audit Outsourcing

There has been a constant need for enterprises to be diligent and conscious about building enhanced transparency given the constantly changing regulations which are critically non-negotiable. Be it the CXOs, the Board, the Stakeholders and Bankers, everyone seeks unshakeable trust when they look at the financial statements. This enhanced financial intelligence and diligence requires enterprises to engage Auditors who are not just experienced but also bring an element of uncompromised Accountability and Controllership. Our Audit services possess a strong understanding of the varied characteristic nature of various enterprises besides economic and regulatory environment. Our engagement mechanism goes beyond Audit and brings value as long-term advisor for the business. Our service delivery endeavors to provide ‘best experience’ to all our audit & assurance clients by delivering service excellence causing minimum disruption and maximized value.

We are in an era where CXOs look for Audit services that go beyond ‘Cost marginalization’ where incremental value and productivity takes equal precedence. It is essential to remain efficient, build value and thrive. Given our industry experience and ‘Big 4’ exposure, our team with Audit expertise and proven methodologies, our niche service delivery aims at bringing tangible solutions for all the audit requirements of any organization. Given the dynamic global scenario, it’s noteworthy that a major part of the Audit can be outsourced with minimal captive intervention which adds value and adds to cost benefits.

SERVICE CAPABILITIES OVERVIEW:

  •   Roll forward of previous year audit file
  •   Preparing working papers for the current year audit
  •    Preliminary analytics
  •    Determination of materiality
  •   For new client/prospective clients prepare offering playbooks
  •   Helping in risk assessment
  •   Drafting engagement letters
  •    Financial statement tie outs
  •   Summarization of Analyst reports, internal audit reports and board minutes
  •   Drafting Management representation letters and opinions
  •   Final analytics
  •   Checklists such as GAAP/GAAS
  •   Draft audit reports
  •    Cash and Bank testing
  •    Accounts Receivable (A/R) testing
  •    Inventory price testing, reconciliations, and tie outs
  •    Property, plant, and equipment testing
  •    Investment testing
  •   Accounts Payable (A/P) testing
  •    Lease testing
  •   Debt testing
  •   Other assets and other liabilities testing
  •   Revenue and Cost of Sales (COS) testing – Test of details
  •    Operating and other expenses testing
  •    Payroll testing
  •   Journal entry profiling and testing
  •   Planning
    •   Roll forward of audit file and preparation of the audit file for the audit
    •   Drafting of any planning letters/ Memos
  •   Reporting
    •   Financial statement tie outs
    •   Assistance in preparing form 5500

  •   Substantive Testing
