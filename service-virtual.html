Virtual CFO Services

For any business, having a core finance officer is essential. But hiring a dedicated Chief Financial Officer (CFO) on board is not just a daunting task, but also a cost intensive role. This becomes a bigger challenge for small and medium sized enterprises, start-ups alike. While it is imperative to have a CFO onboard, it can be done without the conventional style and huge cost involved to have it on the payrolls.

Virtual CFO, can be defined as an outsourced service that offers high quality assistance to startups and SMEs in their day to day financial operations and strategies at an affordable cost that suits the business. Besides the role entailing building strategies to generate value, scout for funding, handling complex plans and structuring related issues, and building sustainable business model, the virtual CFO is as good as the conventional one but with much lower engagement cost.

SERVICE CAPABILITIES OVERVIEW:

  •   Financial Strategy
  •   Short-term and long-term forecasting
  •   Designing financial system
  •   Gap analysis for corporate financial strategies
  •   Strategic interventions
  •   System implementation
  •   Improve financial performance
  •   Facilitating Financial Reports
  •   Interpretation of regulations and impact
  •   Risk mitigation framework
  •   Capital restructuring
  •   Interim CFO services
  •   Cash flow analysis and restructuring
  •   Objective oriented cost cutting measures
  •   Facilitating Merger & Acquisitions
